# Walkthrough

## Hong Kong

Go to the upper floor of the bottom right house and talk to the boy playing the Mega Drive (flag 1)

Go to the upper floor of the top left building (magazine shop) and talk to the manager. (flag 2)

Go in front of Yougekisha (topmost building) and talk to Aguri. An event will play out, and you will enter Yougekisha.

## Yougekisha

Go to the second floor and talk to Ling Ling four times.

Go to the third floor, then to the right and talk to the hooded character with a guitar on his back in the "nap room".

Go back to the second floor and talk to Ling Ling again. Answer yes to all her questions, and she will join you.

## Hong Kong

Talk to the red-dressed girl in the diamond store, or the other one just in front of it.

Go to the bottom right corner and talk to the guard, which will now let you through.

## Kowloon

Talk to the prostitute in front of the upper left building.

Go to the abandoned warehouse to the right in the port.

Talk to the girl in the red dress. Go back towards Kowloon, and an event will play out.

Talk to the man in front of the warehouse. Answer yes to not being a demon and wishing to attending the mass.

Go down into the warehouse basement.

Talk to Raiden (leftmost disciple).

Go to the back room.

Talk to Guru/AkiAki/MeiMei, the founder of Kowloonism. After a few comments she and Raiden will leave for the Kowloon-building.

Go to the building to the left of the locked Kowloon building. Go to the second floor and talk to Dr. Zhang. Answer yes to get the Altar Detector.

Go outside and talk to the people outside the Kowloon building. The back entrance door (hidden) is now unlocked.

Go to the third floor. You can talk to the man with changing personalities. Use the phone to save the game. When ready go down the ladder to enter the first dungeon.
